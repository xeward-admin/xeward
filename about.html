Our Story

At Xeward, our mission is to leverage transformative AI to foster the flourishing of individuals and society as a whole. As GPT emerged, we anticipate rapid progress and the emergence of increasingly capable systems, which may bring forth new challenges. To fulfill our mission, we actively engage in building cutting-edge systems, studying their behaviors, ensuring responsible deployment, and regularly sharing our valuable safety insights. We also collaborate with like-minded projects and stakeholders who share our vision of achieving positive outcomes.

  • Prioritize simplicity and effectiveness
  • Unified as one big team
  • Unparalleled trustworthiness

Xeward represents a collaborative team comprising researchers, engineers, policy experts, business leaders, and operators. Our collective expertise spans diverse domains, allowing us to leverage a wide range of experiences in our work.
